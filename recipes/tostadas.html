Tostada Recipe

Description

These are tostadas! A very tasty hard shell food.

Ingridients

  • Chicken
  • Choice of seasoning
  • Tostada shells
  • Beans
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook chicken in instapot or pan until fully cooked
  2. Cook beans and smash
  3. Shred chicken and season
  4. Spread bean onto tostada shells
  5. Prepare tostadas with choice of toppings
