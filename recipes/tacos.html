Taco Recipe

These are steak tacos!

Ingridients

  • Thin cut steak
  • Seasoning
  • Cilantro
  • Onion
  • Tortillas

Steps

  1. Cook steak unseasoned til brown
  2. Cut steak
  3. Season and cook with 1/2 cup water til water is gone
  4. Cook tortillas
  5. Prepare tacos with steak, cilantro, and onion
